About Me

Hello, my name is Aldous Exequiel S. Damaso. I like to think of myself as the guy who cracks jokes every now and then that really make you laugh your heart out. Currently, I am a college student struggling to keep up with my peers. I feel lost and unsure of what to do with myself. I no longer have fun, I don't vibe with others, and it just doesn't feel like it used to.

Back when I was in high school and senior high school years, I used to have so much fun with others. I would often get called to the guidance office because of it. I even accidentally made a girl cry once when I bounced a volleyball and it hit her face. I got into fights and made unnecessary choices and decisions, but those choices and decisions made me who I am today.

Although the current version of myself may not be the best, I acknowledge that I have gained knowledge and wisdom throughout the years. I am proud to stand beside others and proud of myself for how far I've come. I will look forward to a bright future with a not so bright smile but bright nonetheless. Cheers to you all, most excellent, non-dubious, and non-bogus dudes!
