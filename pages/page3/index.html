My First Project :)))

I recently completed my first project, which was an advocacy video that my friend asked me to help with. It was a very enjoyable experience and it provided a great addition to my portfolio. The video focused on the trust we have with our friends, which develops over the years. It also explored the challenges we face in maintaining this trust, as well as how misunderstandings can break the trust that has been built over many years. This project was my first paid opportunity, and I received 1000 pesos for creating the video.
